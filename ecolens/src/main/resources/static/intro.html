You just finished your favorite meal, at your favorite restaurant. Leaving hardly any remains, you decided that today if the day that you're going to make a difference and recycle. However, as you approach the dreaded waste bins, you stop: What is recyclable? Ecolens is an online tool dedicated to answering that question much faster than your search engine, and educating the user on sustainability in a very approachable and easy to learn format.

How do I use Ecolens?

Using Ecolens is simple, you simply load up the web page, prompt that you would like to start, and then scan your item for more information!
